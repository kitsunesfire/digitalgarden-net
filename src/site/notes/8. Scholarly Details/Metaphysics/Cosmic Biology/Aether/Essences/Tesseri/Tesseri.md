Scholarly Details/Metaphysics/Cosmic Biology/Aether/Essences/Akasha\|Akasha]], the source of all four [[8. Scholarly Details/Metaphysics/Cosmic Biology/Aether/Essences/Tesseri/Tesseri\|Tesseri]].
