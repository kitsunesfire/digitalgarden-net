# Piacqua

[[8. Scholarly Details/Cosmology/Inner Planes/Elemental Planes/Piacqua/Piacqua\|Piacqua]] is a nearly limitless stretch of saline, fresh, and brackish seas, all teeming with oceanic life. While the realm is perfectly amenable to water-breathing creatures, air-breathing travelers must provide their own supply of air or magical means to breathe. Bubbles of breathable atmosphere are relatively rare and securely guarded, anchored over important places of trade and commerce with outsiders.
