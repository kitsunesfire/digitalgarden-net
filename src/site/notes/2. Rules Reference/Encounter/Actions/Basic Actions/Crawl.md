# Crawl
#Move 

**Requirements**. You are [[2. Rules Reference/Conditions/Prone\|Prone]] and your Speed is at least 10 feet.

You move 5 feet by crawling and continue to stay [[2. Rules Reference/Conditions/Prone\|Prone]]
