[[5. Factions/The Asperdrak/The Asperdrak\|The Asperdrak]] are militant extremists who are willing to lay down their lives for the good of the planet. They see themselves as martyrs of [[7. Scholarly Details/Deities/Local Gods/Gaea\|Gaea]] and her last line of defense against the evils that humanity has subjected her to. 

[[5. Factions/The Asperdrak/The Asperdrak\|The Asperdrak]] are typically close combat ambush specialists, trained in primal magic and martial arts. They traditionally utilize a combination of unarmed attacks and monastic weapons, supplemented with natural attacks and other draconic abilities that are believed to be derived from blessings bestowed upon them by [[5. Factions/The Entelexiea/The Entelexiea\|The Entelexiea]]. Those who receive blessings from [[5. Factions/The Entelexiea/The Entelexiea\|The Entelexiea]] are held in high regard, but it is also rumored that these gifts come with a terrible price...
