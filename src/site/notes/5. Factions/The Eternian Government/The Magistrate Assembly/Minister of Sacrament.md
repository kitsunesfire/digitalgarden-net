# Minister of Sacrament

A relatively new position established by [[5. Factions/The Eternian Government/Silas Braska\|Silas Braska]], dedicated to the "preservation of social order" and "defense of moral values" in the [[4. Gazetteer/Eternian Empire\|Eternian Empire]]. Currently held by the [[7. Scholarly Details/Arts & Culture/Titles/Exarch\|Exarch]] of the [[5. Factions/The Celestine/The Celestine\|Church of the Celestine]], [[5. Factions/The Celestine/Andromeda\|Andromeda]]. 

Among other things, the [[5. Factions/The Eternian Government/The Magistrate Assembly/Minister of Sacrament\|Minister of Sacrament]] is responsible for providing counsel, taking confession, imparting divine grace, anointing the sick, and performing various religious ceremonies and rituals such as the [[7. Scholarly Details/Arts & Culture/Festivals & Holidays/Sacramentum\|Sacramentum]].
