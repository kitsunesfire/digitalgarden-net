# Wyrmriders

[[5. Factions/The Imperial Army/The Wyrmriders/The Wyrmriders\|The Wyrmriders]] are a cavalry unit consisting of well-trained soldiers who specialize in riding wyverns in combat scenarios. Based out of [[4. Gazetteer/Lunar Highlands/Castelyr/Castelyr\|Castelyr]], their training exercises can occasionally be viewed up and down the southern coasts of Zarthura and throughout the peaks of the [[4. Gazetteer/Lunar Highlands/Lunar Highlands\|Lunar Highlands]].
