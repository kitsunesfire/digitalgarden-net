[[5. Factions/The Imperial Army/The Solar Justiciars/The Solar Justiciars\|The Solar Justiciars]] are holy defenders and protectors of [[0. Archive/Old World Atlas/Zarthura\|Zarthura]]. In addition to their combat training, they are required to spend a great deal of time studying at the [[5. Factions/The Imperial Academy/The Imperial Academy\|The Imperial Academy]]. While they receive a broad education on a variety of creatures and threats, [[5. Factions/The Imperial Army/The Solar Justiciars/The Solar Justiciars\|justiciars]] typically specialize in eliminating [[3. Demographics/Ancestries/Apparition/Devil\|devils]], [[3. Demographics/Ancestries/Apparition/Demon\|demons]], [[3. Demographics/Ancestries/Wyrmkin/Dragon\|dragons]], [[3. Demographics/Versatile Heritages/Vampyr\|vampyr]], and other monsters of the [[7. Scholarly Details/Cosmology/Cosmic Biology/Aether/Essences/Polarities/Void\|Void]]. [[5. Factions/The Imperial Army/The Solar Justiciars/The Solar Justiciars\|The Solar Justiciars]] frequently collaborate with [[5. Factions/The Imperial Army/The Suncasters/The Suncasters\|The Suncasters]], especially in efforts related to stabilizing the [[4. Gazetteer/Red Wastes/Red Wastes\|Red Wastes]] after a [[Dark Sun\|Dark Sun]] has passed.

In addition to their monster hunting, [[5. Factions/The Imperial Army/The Solar Justiciars/The Solar Justiciars\|justiciars]] are commonly trained in legal matters and may act as law enforcement or contract negotiators in major Zarthurian settlements. Generally speaking, if a Zarthurian wants to hire a private investigator or a lawyer, chances are they will seek out a [[5. Factions/The Imperial Army/The Solar Justiciars/The Solar Justiciars\|justiciar]]. For example, in instances where an [[5. Factions/The Imperial Army/The Imperial Army\|The Imperial Army]] soldier may have been bribed to look the other way, a [[5. Factions/The Imperial Army/The Solar Justiciars/The Solar Justiciars\|justiciar]] might be brought in to investigate and have justice served. However, these services do not come cheap-- [[5. Factions/The Imperial Army/The Solar Justiciars/The Solar Justiciars\|The Solar Justiciars]] are highly qualified specialists, after all, and they typically charge private citizens a hefty sum for their time.
