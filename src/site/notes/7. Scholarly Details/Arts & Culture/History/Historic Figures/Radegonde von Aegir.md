# Radegonde Von Aegir

Commonly known as "Radegonde the Righteous", [[7. Scholarly Details/Arts & Culture/History/Historic Figures/Radegonde von Aegir\|Radegonde von Aegir]] was a Zarthurian [[7. Scholarly Details/Arts & Culture/Titles/Monarch/Kaiser\|Kaiser]] who is mostly famous for his successful campaign against the [[5. Factions/The Luminari/The Luminari\|The Luminari]]. It was under his command that the [[5. Factions/The Luminari/The Luminari\|The Luminari]] were erased from [[0. Archive/Old World Atlas/Zarthura\|Zarthura]] and their [[7. Scholarly Details/Arts & Culture/Titles/Spiritualist\|Spiritualist]] practices were made [[7. Scholarly Details/Arts & Culture/Legislation/Illegal Magic\|illegal]].
